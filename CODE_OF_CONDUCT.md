# Code of conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: everyone is welcome here. Be kind, be patient, and assume good intent. Don't harass, insult, or belittle others.

If someone behaves in a way that goes against this, or you experience something that makes you uncomfortable, please let the maintainer know at **songbird@tuta.com**. Reports are handled privately. You will get a response within a reasonable time (usually a few days).

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 is available at the link above and applies here in full.
